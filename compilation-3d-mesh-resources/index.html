Compilation of 3D mesh models

Awesome compilation of 3D mesh model datasets and resources for research purposes.

Apr 14, 2021 • Zeeshan Khan Suri • 2 mins read

A compilation of 3D mesh models found on the web for research purposes.

Disclaimer: All images shown here are the sole property of their respective owners. I do not claim any Copyright/authority over them.

Note: This list is not regularly updated and is looking for maintainers. If you want to include some dataset, please open a Pull Request.
