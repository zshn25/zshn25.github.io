Compilation of 3D mesh models

Awesome compilation of 3D mesh model datasets and resources for research purposes.

Apr 14, 2021 • Zeeshan Khan Suri • 2 mins read

#3d-mesh   #datasets   #computer-vision

A compilation of 3D mesh models found on the web for research purposes.

Disclaimer: All images shown here are the sole property of their respective owners. I do not claim any Copyright/authority over them.

Note: This list is not regularly updated and is looking for maintainers. If you want to include some dataset, please open a Pull Request.

  1. OmniObject3D, 2023: 6k objects in 190 categories

  2. Objaverse, 2023: 800K+ Annotated 3D Objects

  3. Amazon Berkeley Objects (ABO) Dataset, 2022

  4. BuildingNet: Learning to Label 3D Buildings, 2k buildings with surface labels

  5. 3D-FUTURE: 3D FUrniture shape with TextURE, 2021

  6. Co3D: Common Objects in 3D, 2021

  7. AKB-48 Articulation Objects

  8. Toys4K, 2021: 4k objects

  9. Hypersim, 2021

  10. Habitat-Matterport 3D Research Dataset (HM3D), Link2, Link3, 2021

  11. Omnidata, Link2, 2021

  12. TASKONOMY Dataset, 2021

  13. BlendedMVS, 2020

  14. Scanned Objects by Google Research, 2020

  15. ScanObjectNN: Revisiting Point Cloud Classification: A New Benchmark Dataset and Classification Model on Real-World Data, 2019: 15k real scanned objects

  16. Replica, ReplicaCAD, 2019

  17. ABC Dataset, 2019
