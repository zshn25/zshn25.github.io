## Privacy

Your privacy matters. This site is designed to respect it:

- **No cookies** are set by this site
- **No analytics or tracking** (Google Analytics is disabled)
- **All CSS, fonts, and scripts are self-hosted** -- no requests to third-party CDNs (Cloudflare, jsDelivr, Google Fonts)
- **Referrer policy** is set to `no-referrer` -- external sites cannot see where you came from
- **AI crawlers are blocked** via robots.txt (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, CCBot, etc.)
- **Third-party embeds** (YouTube, comments) are **opt-in only** -- they load only when you explicitly click
- YouTube uses privacy-enhanced mode (`youtube-nocookie.com`) with `referrerpolicy=origin` to limit tracking
- Social embeds use `data-dnt="true"` (Do Not Track)
- Our World in Data charts load directly (they are cookieless and privacy-respecting by design)

Read more about [why privacy matters]({{ site.baseurl }}/why-privacy-matters/).
